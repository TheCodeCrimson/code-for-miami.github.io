Join CodeAcross America developers, designers, data geeks, leaders, and idea-makers who volunteer to help Miami-Dade's government and civic organizations adopt open web technologies.

CodeAcross 2015

Saturday, February 21st @ 9 a.m.

CodeAcross is a weekend of civic hacking events hosted around the world by nodes of the Code for America network. CodeAcross is timed to coincide with both the last weekend of the Code for America Fellows residency and International Open Data Day.

This year's theme for CodeAcross 2015 is Principles for 21st Century Government.

Who should get involved? Urbanists, civic hackers, government staff, developers, designers, community organizers, and anyone with the passion to make their city better.

Breakfast and lunch provided. Come hack with your neighbors!

Location: Miami Ad School, 571 NW 28th St.

Time: 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
